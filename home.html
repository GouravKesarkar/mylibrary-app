✨ Welcome to My Book Shelf!

Hey everyone!
I had quite a few books lying around in my cupboard and thought, why not share them instead of letting them gather dust? 😊
So, I created this app where you can browse the catalogue, book, and borrow one book at a time — totally free!
You and your kids are welcome to explore and read.

If you’d like, you can also add your own books to the collection.
My only request — please take care of the books and return them in the same condition so others can enjoy them too.

Happy reading! 📚✨
